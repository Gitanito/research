Coming Day, The.
1891-1911 Monthly
London, England. Publisher: Williams & Norgate. Editor: John Page Hopps.
1/1, January 1891. 16 pp. Hopps was a Unitarian minister and general reformer. He had earlier started Daybreak, which formed the basis of the far more interesting Medium and Daybreak, and also published several Christian reform journals. Harvard University; University of Texas, Austin; GTU, Berkeley; National Library of Scotland; University of Manchester Library.
