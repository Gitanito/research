    Periodical: Journal de la Societe Pgrenologique de Paris

Summary  From Pat Deveney's database:

Journal de la Societe Phrenologique de Paris.
1832--1835 Quarterly
Paris, France. Language: French. Publisher: Societe Phrenologique de Paris, J.-B. Balliere. Editor: Re edige e par une commission de ses membres.
1/1, 1832-1835. 96-112 pp., 12-15 francs a year.

The prospectus for the journal advises that its purpose is to expand phrenology beyond what had already been discovered and to make its value known and available to artists: it alone could guide the hand of the artist and the sculptor and bring rationality to their work. University of Chicago; Harvard; BNF.
