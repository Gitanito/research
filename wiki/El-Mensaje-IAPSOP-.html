    Periodical: El Mensaje

Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Mensaje, El.
Organo oficial de la Sociedad Teosofica en Uruguay / Revista Uruguana de Teosofia.
Other titles: Revista de la Federacion Teosofica del Uruguay
1925-1926? Monthly
Montevideo, Uruguay. Language: Spanish. Editor: Mario Radarelli Panzacchi; "Dr. Jehel, S.I.".
Succeeds: Revista de la Federación Teosófica del Uruguay
1/1, October 1925-December 1926(?) 16-24 pp., free to members of the Theosophical Society in Uruguay.

The journal began life as Revista de la Federacion Teosofica del Uruguay in 1924 and then become El Mensaje. This was a standard Adyar Theosophical magazine. "Jehel" (Leo Alvarez Costet de Mascheville) is said to have been active in editing the journal. On him and his activities in Martinism, see the note under Iniciacion.
