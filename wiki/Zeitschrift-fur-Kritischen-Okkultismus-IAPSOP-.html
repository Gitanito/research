    Periodical: Zeitschrift fur Kritischen Okkultismus

Summary  From Pat Deveney's database:

Zeitschrift fur Kritischen Okkultismus und Grenzfragen des Seelenlebens.
1925-1928 Quarterly
Stuttgart, Germany. Language: German. Publisher: Verlag von Ferdinand Enke. Editor: Richard Baerwald (1867-1929).
1/1, 1925-1928. 80 pp.

This is said to have been an attempt to provide a scientific, "unparteiisch" forum for both advocates and debunkers of parapsychology. Contributions by Max Dessoir, Rudolph Tischner, E.J. Dingwall, Schrenck Notzing and others.
