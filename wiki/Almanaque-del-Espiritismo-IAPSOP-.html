    Periodical: Almanaque del Espiritismo

Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Almanaque del Espiritismo.
Other titles: Almanaque El Criterio Espiritista
1873--1875? Annual
Madrid, Spain. Language: Spanish. Publisher: Jose Martin: Sociedad Espiritista Espanola / Imp. Jose Martin Alcantara. Editor: Alfredo Perea and Carlos Capuz.
1/1, 1873-1875(?)

This was put out by the group that published El Criterio Espiritista and bears traces of the ideological split that was brewing within the Grupo Marietta/Sociedad Espiritista Espanola by its inclusion of articles on D.D. Home and other phenomenal mediums. Biographical articles, nicely illustrated with plates, of the leading Spanish spiritists, D.D. Home, Cora Tappan, Gerald Massey, et al. Noted in Florentino Barerra, El Auto de Fe de Barcelona, 3d ed. (Buenos Aires: Ediciones Vida Infinita, 2008), 53. Biblioteca Nacional de Espana; Hemeroteca Municipal de Madrid.
