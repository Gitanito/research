    Periodical: Old Moore's Almanack

Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Old Moore's Almanac.
Vox Stellarum.
Other titles: Vox Stellarum, etc.
1701-1916? Annual
London, England. Publisher: W. Foulsham & Co. . Editor: Francis Moore, Tycho Wing, Henry Andrews, et al.

Longest running astrological almanac in the world. It began in 1701 as Francis Moore's Old Moore: An Ancient Almanac, Offering Astrological Forces and Determinants. A monthly companion, Old Moore's Monthly Messenger, was started in 1907.

Issues:Old Moore's Almanack 1916
