    Periodical: Weltmerism

Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Weltmerism.
Devoted to the Promulgation of Truth, the Healing of the Sick and the Instruction of All Who Desire to Learn Self-Mastery.
1901? Bimonthly
Nevada, MO. Publisher: Weltmer School of Healing. Editor: Sidney A. Weltmer; S.W. Weltmer, first assistant.
Succeeds: Magnetic Journal (1898-1903?); Weltmer Journal (1906?); Fulfillment (1906) Succeeded by: New Thought (Chicago)
24 pp., fifty cents. Illustrated. This was one of the advertising vehicles for Sidney A. Weltmer’s psychic healing courses and sanitarium, filled with glad testimonials from satisfied patients, advertisements for courses, and glowing biographies of the Weltmer School of Healing. On Weltmer, see the note under Weltmer’s Magazine. The issue of April 1901 is in the Weltmer paper at the State Historical Society of Missouri. Issues:Weltmerism Nov 1900 Weltmerism Apr 1901
