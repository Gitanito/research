Nueva Era, La.
Revista Mensual de Estudios Psicologicos.
1912--1914? Weekly (?), then monthly
Sabadell (Barcelona), Spain. Language: Spanish. Editor: Angel Aguarod, Eduardo Pascual, Fabian Palasi.
Corporate author: Organo Official de la Federacion Espiritista Espa√±ola1/1, August 1912. 20 pp., 3 pesetas a year in Spain and 6 elsewhere.

This was a Kardecist spiritist journal. Aguarod, its founder, was a prolific and tireless propagandist. He had earlier founded and directed El Espiritismo in Buenos Aires (1905-1912), and went on to direct Union Espiritista (Barcelona), Fraternidad (Alicante), La Antorcha del Progresso (Badalona), Eternidade and Boletim da Federacao Espirita do Rio Grande do Sul, while contributing to Luz y Union, Luz del Porvenir,Constancia, Fraternidad, Rosendo (Cuba), and others.
