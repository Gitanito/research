Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Theosophia.
A Living Philosophy for Humanity.
1944-1981 Bimonthly, then quarterly
Los Angeles, CA. Editor: Boris de Zirkoff.
1/1, May-June 1944-1981.

De Zirkoff (1902-1981), who claimed to have been the second cousin once removed of Blavatsky, was the assembler of her Collected Writings. Contributions by or excerpts from H.P. Blavatsky, W.Q. Judge, G. de Purucker, Katherine Tingley, Mary K. Neff, H.S. Olcott, Iverson Harris, Jacob Bongren, AE (George W. Russel), et al. Reprinted in 4 vols., Edmonton Theosophical Society, Alberta, Canada. NYPL; San Diego State University; University of California, San Diego and Santa Barbara; Emory University; Theosophical Society, Wheaton, IL; Wayne State University; National Library of Australia.
