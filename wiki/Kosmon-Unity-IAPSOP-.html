    Periodical: Kosmon Unity

Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Kosmon Unity.
A Magazine for All Interested in the Light of the New Age.
1904 Quarterly
London, England. Publisher: Kosmon Press. Editor: A. Bridger, President of the Kosmon Extension.
24 pp. 4s. 6d. a year. The journal is dated both in Gregorian style and as “A.K.” (Anno Kosmon) beginning with the year 1848.

Martin Gardner in Urantia notes that this journal had been started in 1904. If that is so, it was revived in a new series in 1946. This was the journal of the Kosmon Church and Fraternity of Faithists in England, designed both to spread the word of Kosmon to non-believers and to expound the details of John Newbrough's Oahspe to members. Skidmore Library, Lily Dale (2 issues).
