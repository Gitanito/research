    Periodical: The Cassadagan

Summary: From Pat Deveney's database:

Cassadagan, The. A Monthly Magazine Devoted to the Interests of Spiritualism in America.. Seeing we also are compassed about by so great a cloud of witnesses 1891-1897 Monthly for 10 months a year; monthly Meadville, PA; then Seaforth, Ontario, Canada. Publisher: Cassadaga Lake Free Association. Editor: F.H. Bemis, then W.O. Washburn, then Lyman C. Howe and F.G. Neelan. Succeeded by: Sunflower

1/1, 1891-1900 (?) Fifty then 25 and then 50 cents a year, 4-6 pp. Journal for the annual summer camp meeting at Lily Dale on Lake Cassadaga, New York. In 1897 the journal's affairs were taken over by F.G. Neelin/Nealan, a Canadian publisher, and the journal moved to Ontario. It was still being published, as noted in the Religio-Philosophical Journal, in the beginning of 1900 and was then replaced by the more substantial Sunflower. NY State Library; SUNY, Fredonia; NSAC, Lily Dale; Skidmore, Lily Dale (3 issues). Issues:Cassadagan July 25-August 21 1890 Cassadagan V1 N5 June 2 1891 Cassadagan V4 N11 July 17 1894 Cassadagan V5 N6 Feb 1895
