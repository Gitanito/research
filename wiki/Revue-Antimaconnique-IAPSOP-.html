Revue Antimaconnique.
1910--1916 Monthly
Paris, France. Language: French. Editor: Commandant Cuignet. Corporate author: Ligue francaise antimaconnique
1/1, November 1910-1916. 24 fr. a year in France, 27 fr. elsewhere.

The purpose of the Ligue Francaise Antimaconnique, the publisher of this journal, was to diffuse broadly among the populace a knowledge of the insidious efforts of the Freemasons internationally to undermine the traditional Christian culture of the West. A necessary part of this investigative effort involved occultism, which the journal regularly documented, and the labyrinthine ways the secret societies and the occult were doing the work of Masonry. BNF.
