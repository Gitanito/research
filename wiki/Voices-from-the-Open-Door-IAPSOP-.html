    Periodical: Voices from the Open Door

Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Voices from the Open Door.
1912 Monthly
Cleveland, OH. Publisher: Open Door Publishing Co. / Eben Fish. Editor: Margaret J. Houts.
1/1, 1912.

This was a series rather than a journal. The preface to the first volume says that this "is a plain narrative of facts as related by reliable, intelligent people that have laid off their natural bodies and passed into the inner world." Houts (1838- ) was the author with Margaret Scott Houts of Messages from the Beyond: Narrated Experiences of Life After Death, reprinted 1962. LOC; Yale University; Oberlin College; Western Reserve Historical Society; University of Oregon.
