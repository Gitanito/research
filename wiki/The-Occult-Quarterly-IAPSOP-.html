    Periodical: The Occult Quarterly

Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Occult Quarterly, The.
A Magazine For Spiritual Illumination.
1934--1935 Quarterly
Chicago, IL. Editor: Royal Eugene Parks.
1/1, January-March, 1934. 50 cents a year, 16 pp.

Parks ("Lecturer, Teacher, Healer") serialized his "Lessons on psychic unfoldment" in the journal and ran a Silent Healing Center from the same address as the journal, where he also sold incense, candles, and the like. Contribution by Effa Danielson (publisher of the Occult Digest), and advertisements for fringe mediums, "Emissaries of the Divine Light," healers, psychic readers, spiritualist counselors, and numerologists of the time.
