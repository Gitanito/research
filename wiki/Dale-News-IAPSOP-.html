Dale News, The.
To be a Spiritualist is to Know Spiritualism as a Science, to Accept it as a Philosophy, to Practice it as a Religion
1933-1938?
Lily Dale, NY, and Cassadaga, FL.
Publisher: R.G. Pressing.
1/1, 1933-1938(?) Vol. 3, no. 2 is 1935.
4 pp., 5 cents a copy.

Camp meeting notice of Lily Dale, New York (summer) and Cassadaga, Florida (winter) spiritualist camp meetings. Short biographies and notices of featured lecturers and mediums and local advertisements for services that might be of interest to camp attendees. SUNY Fredonia (1935-6, 1938).
