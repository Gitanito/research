Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Zeitschrift fur Psychische Arzte.
Mit besonderer Berucksichtigung des Magnetismus.
1818-1822
Quarterly
Leipzig, Germany.
Language: German.
Editor: Christian Friedrich Nasse.
Publisher: Cnobloch.
Succeeded by: Zeitschrift / Jahrbucher fur Anthropologie
1/1, 1818-1822.
Illustrated.

Nasse (1778-1851)was a physician and proto-psychiatrist with a special interest in the understanding of the physical aspects of mental disorders, and was also a leading contributor to the Archiv fur den thierischen Magnetismus. Contributions by Adolph Carl August Eschenmayer, and Joseph Ennemoser. Crabtree 291.
