    Periodical: The New Way

Summary:   From Pat Deveney's database:

New Way, The.
Illustrated Monthly.
The Best Is Yet to Come
1911--1929 Monthly
Point Loma, CA.
Editor: Katherine Tingley.
Succeeded by: Theosophical Path (incorporated into, 1929)
Corporate author: International Theosophical League of Humanity 1/1, 1911-1929. 8 pp.

Free "for prisoners and others whether behind the bars or not." Part of Tingley's Theosophical work for prisoners, although the journal appears rather highbrow for the expected audience. University of California.
