    Periodical: Veritable Almanach Astrologique

Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Veritable Almanach Astrologique, Le.
d'apres les fideles traditions et les donnees exactes de la science.
1910 Annual
Paris, France. Language: French. Publisher: Librairie du Merveilleux. Editor: F. Ch. Barlet (Albert Faucheux).
1910. 100 pp.

Provided a calendar for the year, situating the year in its place in the Julian, Chinese, Jewish, Muslim, ecclesiastical, and Chinese calendars, and threw in ephemerides, horoscopes of various rulers, discussion of harmonical and discordant aspects and, for good measure, the proper astrological basis of talismans . BNF. Microfilm
