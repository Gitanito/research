Liberty.
Not the Daughter But the Mother of Order / The Pioneer Organ of Anarchism.
1881—1907 Monthly
Boston, MA. Editor: Benjamin R. Tucker, Stephen Pearl Andrews, co-editor.
1/1, 1881-1907. This was Tucker’s very influential individualist anarchist journal. It is included here because so many spiritualists participated: Andrews, Juliet Severance, Lois Waisbrooker, et al. Tucker also assisted in editing Ezra Heywood’s The Word.
