Until its destruction by fire in 1939, Borley Rectory, a Victorian building in Essex in southern England, was the focus of persistent claims of poltergeist and haunting phenomena.  Cheltenham Ghost

This 1892 report describes frequent sightings by multiple witnesses of an apparitional figure, a woman dressed in black, by members of a household in Cheltenham in the south-west of England.  Cock Lane Ghost
