    Periodical: La Rose Croix

Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Rose + Croix, La .
Catalogue du Salon de la Rose-Croix / Ordre de la Rose-Croix du Temple et du Graal / Geste Esthetique / Catalogue des Oeuvres Exposees.
1892-1897? Called Monthly or Quarterly, but actually Annual
Paris, France. Language: French. Publisher: Commanderie de Tiphereth. Editor: Josephin Peladan.
1/1, 1892-1897(?) 5 francs a year.

This was in effect the catalogue of Sar Peladan's salons and Gestes Esthetiques in Paris in the 1890s, though it also served as a journal and advertisement for his Ordre de la Rose + Croix de Temple et du Graal, as well. Peladan was a prominent Symbolist artist of the fin de siecle, with a strong occult bent. Peladan had originally moved in the circle of Stanislas de Guaita's Rose-Croix kabbalistique but in 1890, principally because of his own strong Catholicism, he had abandoned de Guaita and his work and started his own salons. ZDB: Stuttgart WLB; BNF.
