Bi-Monthly Newsletter.
1972--1981 Bimonthly
Elsinore, CA.
Publisher: El Cariso Publications; Life Understanding Foundation.
Editor: Bill Cox and Georgiana Teeple, editors and publishers.
1/1, September-October 1972-9/5, no. 52, March-April 1981.
$2.75-$9.00 a year, 8 pp.

Some writers imply that if certain rigid instructions are not followed, the replica's self-generating power will fall to sharpen razor blades or mummify pieces of meat").
