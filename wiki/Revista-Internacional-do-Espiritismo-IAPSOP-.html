1925 Monthly
Matao, E. S. Paolo, Brazil. Language: Portuguese. Publisher: Centro Espirita "Amantes da Pobreza". Editor: Cairbar de Souza Schutel; Jose da Costa Filho, A. Watson Campelo. Corporate author: Centro Espirita "Amantes da Pobreza"
1/1, February 1925-current. 24-32 pp., 24$ a year.

Kardecist spiritist journal with a considerable admixture of articles on famous spiritualists and investigators like Victor Hugo, Catulle Mendes, Gustave Geley, and notes on spiritualism and psychical research around the world. Schutel had been the founder of O Clarim. The journal was largely composed of excerpts from other Kardecist journals around the world and was intended as the more scholarly companion of the popular O Clarim. NYPL; LOC; University of Florida; University of Texas, Austin; Princeton Theological Seminary.
