Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

British Mazdaznan Magazine.
The Official Organ of the Mazdaznan Association in Great Britain.
1925-1938? Monthly
Manchester, then London, England. Editor: Lt.-Col. Arthur F. Gault.
1/1, 1925. 6/- a year, 48-68 pp.

This was published in Manchester by the association that in 1914-1915 had published a quarterly journal of the same name. Devoted to the teachings of Dr. O.Z. Ha'nish. Universiteit van Amsterdam; Utrecht University Library. Issues:British Mazdaznan Magazine V10 N7 Mar 1934 British Mazdaznan Magazine V14 N10 Jun 1938
