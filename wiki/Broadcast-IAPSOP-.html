Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Broadcast.
A Non-sectarian Monthly Magazine Devoted to the publication of Spiritual Truths along Ethical, Philosophical and Religious Lines.
1922-1923 Monthly
Los Angeles, CA. Editor: Walter N. Goldschmidt.
1/1, October 1922. 32 pp., $2.00 a year.

Contributions by Manly P. Hall, Franz Hartmann, James M. Warnack, Henry Christeen Warnack, and others, together with discussions of Indian and Chinese philosophy as understood in the light of New Thought.
