    Periodical: The Diamond

Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Diamond.
For Those Who Read To Succeed / A Weekly Inspirational Message.
1930
Editor: Edna Ballard.
Succeeds: American Occultist
1/1, 1930. 12 pp., 25 cents a copy, $8.00 a year.

This was the successor of Edna Ballard's American Occultist (q.v.) and continued its content, format and volume numbering. It was begun in fall 1930 and probably expired soon after. LOC. Issues:Diamond V1 N21 Nov 1930
