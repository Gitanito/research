Herald of Light, The.
1857-1861 Monthly
New York, NY. Publisher: New Church Publishing Association. Editor: Thomas Lake Harris and Rev. M.C.C. Church.
1/1, May 1857-August 1, 1861. $1.50, then $2.00 a year. New Church, Swedenborgian. When Harris left for London in May 1859, Marius C. C. Church carried on the paper alone.
