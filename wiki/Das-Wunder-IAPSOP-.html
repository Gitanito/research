    Periodical: Das Wunder

Summary:   From Pat Deveney's database:

Wunder, Das.
Zeitschrift fur Astrologie, Okkultismus, Magie, Spiritismus und verwandte Gebiete, herausgegeben unter Mitwirkung anerkannter Vertreter auf geisteswissenschaftlichem Gebiete.
1927-1929
Frankfort am Main, then Memmingen, Germany. Language: German. Publisher: Verlags- und Druckereigenoss.
1/1, 1927-1929. 136-148 pp, 8 x 10. Color plates of original drawings and paintings both within the journal and as its covers, and photographs.

Pretensions of erudition, with many contributions by those prefacing their names with "Herr Professor Doctor" and the like, and articles by Henri Birven, "G.W. Surya," and Ferdinand Maack. Considerable astrological content. Hints of things to come in, e.g., P.S.E. Sohmann's "Echte Deutsche Mystik" in the first issue. Universiteit Utrecht; ZDB: Freiburg Inst Grenzgeb Psychol; Munchen UB; Nurnberg GermNatMus WGL; Berlin SBB Haus Unter d.Linden; Berlin SBB Haus Potsdamer Str; Frankfurt/M Inst Gesch Med; Halle/S ZwB Theologie; Dresden SLUB, ZB; Universiteit Utrecht; DNB.
