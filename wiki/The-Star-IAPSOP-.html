    Periodical: The Star

Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Star, The.
1928 Monthly
Los Angeles, CA. Editor: J. Krishnamurti, president of the International Editorial Board, and John A. Ingelman and Marie Russak Hotchner as editors of the American National Editorial Board.
1/1, January 1928. 64 pp.

This was the official organ of the Order of The Star. It was published centrally with news and activities of the order internationally, and then distributed to individual countries where it was translated into the local language and carried local news of the order. Filled with encomiums for Krishnamurti and snippets of his sayings and poetry. NSAC, Lily Dale (1 issue).
