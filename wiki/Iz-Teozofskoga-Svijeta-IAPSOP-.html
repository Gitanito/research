    Periodical: Iz Teozofskoga Svijeta

Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Iz Teozofskoga Svijeta [From the Theosophical World].
1938-1939? Zagreb, Yugoslavia Language: Croatian. Publisher: Zagreb Theosophical Society of Yugoslavia.
1/1, February 1939-9-10, October-November 1939. Theosophical journal adhering to the Adyar Theosophical Society. Typed and mimeographed sheets. Excerpts from articles by C. Jinarajadasa, George Arundale, et al., as well as material from the society in Yugoslavia, book reviews, etc.

From Nemanja Radulovic: A kind of bulletin (around 10 pages each). Translations of Theosophical texts; news from the Theosophical worls in Yugoslavia and abroad. Available at http://www.jivatma.si/gradiva/starejse-revije-casopisi/iz-teozofskog-svijeta (incomplete).

Copies preserved by Nemanja Radulovic.
