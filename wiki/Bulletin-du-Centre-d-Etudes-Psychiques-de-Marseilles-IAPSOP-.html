Marseilles, France. Language: French.
Corporate author: Centre d'Etudes Psychiques de Marseilles/Societe d'Etudes Psychiques de Marseilles
1/1, 1902-1913. 12-24 pp., 0 fr 50 an issue, 2-6 francs a year, with subscription included in dues of the Centre/Societe. This was a regional journal of parapsychology, devoted primarily to publishing notable conferences in the field and extensive critical reviews of current literature. Noted in Revue Spirite, January 1, 1904. BNF.
