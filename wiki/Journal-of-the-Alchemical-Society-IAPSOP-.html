    Periodical: Journal of the Alchemical Society

Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Journal of the Alchemical Society, The.
1913-1915 Monthly
London, England. Publisher: H.K. Lewis. Editor: H. Stanley Redgrove.
Corporate author: Alchemical Society
1/1, 1913-1915. 12 pp.

This was an early semi-scholarly journal devoted to the history of alchemy. The Alchemical Society had been founded in 1912 by Stanley Redgrove, W. Gorn Old, and others and viewed alchemy broadly to include extensive reviews of kabbalism, Rosicrucianism, modern alchemy and occultism generally. NYPL; University of Chicago, University of Wisconsin; J.R. Ritman Library; Glasgow University; University of Leeds; Yale University; etc.
