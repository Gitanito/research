    Periodical: Spiritual Truth

Summary:   From Pat Deveney's database:

Spiritual Truth.
An Independent Journal of Spiritual Progress, Perception, Research, Discussion and Testing.
1922-1926? Weekly
London, England.
1/1, September 15, 1922. 8 pp., threehalfpence a copy, 8s. a year.

This was a Christian spiritualist journal, notable only because it had several contributions by Arthur Conan Doyle. 1/1, September 13, 1922, NSAC, Lily Dale and 1/2, September 20, 1922-4/84, March 17, 1926), Skidmore Library, Lily Dale.
