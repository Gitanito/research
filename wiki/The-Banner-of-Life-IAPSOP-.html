    Periodical: The Banner of Life

Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Banner of Life, The.
An Exponent of Spiritual and Liberal Thought and Facts.
Other titles: Spiritual Era
1910--1935 Weekly
Boston, MA. Editor: Advisory Board, Frederick W. Taylor, President; T.A. Scott; literary editor, A.G. Cupid.
Corporate author: Official Organ of the Massachusetts State Association of Spiritualists1/1, 1910-May 25, 1935. $1.00 a year. 4 pp., 14 1/2 x 22.

The volumes for September 5, 1925-January 1, 1927 are titled Spiritual Era. This is one of the few purely spiritualist journals of the period and is a good example of the sad decay of the movement in the twentieth century. It provided platitudinous commentary on the spirits, a "Mediums' Directory," and "Reports of Societies," but the result lacks energy and is padded out with recipes and other general material. NYPL; LOC. Issues:Banner of Life V5 N209 Oct 3 1914 Banner of Life V5 N210 Oct 10 1914
