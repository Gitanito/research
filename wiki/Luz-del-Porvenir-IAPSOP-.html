    Periodical: Luz del Porvenir

Summary:   From Pat Deveney's database:

Luz del Porvenir.
Mensuario Ecléctico de Propaganda Espirita y Filosofica (1914) / Revista Popular de Estudios Psicologicos y Ciencias (1926) / Revista Popular de Estudios Psicologicos y Ciencias Afines (1930) / Organo del Centro La Buena Nueva / Organo de la Federacion Espiritista Espanola.
1913—1936 Weekly, then monthly
Barcelona (Gracia), Spain.
Language: Spanish.
Editor: Santiago Arnando; Salvador Vendrell Xucla; Juan Torras Serra; F. Corchon; B. Boorques; Salvador Vendrell Xucia. Succeeds: Luz del Porvenir (1879)
Corporate author: Centro La Buena Nueva / Federación Espiritista Española 1/1, January 1913-October 1936. 32-36 pp. (varies), 4-30 pesetas in Barcelona, 5 in the remainder of Spain and in Portugal, and 6 outside the peninsula.

This was a re-animation of the earlier journal of the same name founded by Amalia Domingo Soler (1835-1909) in 1879, but displayed the changes that had overtaken the spiritualist movement in the interval with articles on psychic force, Theosophy, orientalism, and the like. The final issues in 1936 called upon spiritualists to support the republic. Noted in Oscar M. Garcia Rodriguez, Historia del Periodismo Espiritista en Espana; De Kardec Hasta la Guerra Civil Espanola (2006), online at http://www.ameporto.org/pt/entrevistas/oscar.pdf. Archivo General de la Guerra Civil Espanola.
