Theosophische Forum, Das.
Deutsche Abteilung der Theosophischen Gesellschaft.
1930--1951
Stuttgart, Germany. Language: German. Publisher: Deutsche Abteilung der Theosophischen Gesellschaft. Editor: G. Saalfrank (?).
1/1, January 1930-1935; 1948-1951. 8-16 pp. Included with membership dues.

This was the organ of the German section of Katherine Tingley's Theosophical Society, originally at Point Loma, California, under the leadership of Dr. G. von Purucker. ZDB: Heidelberg UB; Munchen BSB; Berlin SBB Haus Unter d.Linden; Frankfurt/M DNB; Leipzig DNB; Weimar HAAB.
