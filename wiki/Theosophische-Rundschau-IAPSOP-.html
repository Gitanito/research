    Periodical: Theosophische Rundschau

Summary: From Pat Deveney's database:

Theosophische Rundschau.
Publikations-Organ der deutschredenden Mitglieder der Theosophischen Gesellschaft, des internationalen Bundes der (freien) theosophischen Vereine.
1902--1907 Monthly
Leipzig, Germany.
Language: German.
Editor: Arthur Weber.
Publisher: Theosophische Central-Buchhandlung Leipzig (Hugo Vollrath).
1/1, 1902-1906(?) 4-8pp.

This was a publication of Franz Hartmann's I.T.V. and was originally printed as a supplement to Weber's Theosophischer Wegweiser. Its purpose was to report on the details of "the activities of Theosophical associations at home and abroad," containing speaking schedules, changes in officers, recent and ephemeral publications, etc. The Neue Metaphysische Rundschau, 1/5-7 (December 1897-February 1898): 97, lists another journal of this same name, perhaps a predecessor, published at Zehlendorf by Paul Zillman in 1898. ZDB: Freiburg UB; Erlangen-N UB; Leipzig DNB; Archivo General de la Guerra Civil Espanola.
