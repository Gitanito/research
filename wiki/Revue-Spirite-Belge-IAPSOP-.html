Revue Spirite Belge, La.
La Vie d'Outre-Tombe.
1894 Monthly
Liege, Belgium. Language: French. Editor: Charles Fritz; J. L'homme.
Succeeds: Vie d'Outre-Tombe Corporate author: Organe Officiel de l'Union Spirite Belge
1/1, January 1894-current. 20 pp., 15 francs (Belgian) a year, abroad 4 belgas.

This was the Organe Officiel de l'Union Spirite Belge. It carried articles and excerpts from most francophone spiritist authors, but was devoted largely to the doings of congresses and local meetings and news.
