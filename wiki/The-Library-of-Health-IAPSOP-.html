Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Library of Health, The.
1897-1900 Monthly
New York, NY. Publisher: Alliance Publishing Company. Editor: Charles Brodie Patterson.
1/1, May 1897-April 1900. 16 pp., $1.00 a year. The journal was a platitudinous series of ruminations by Patterson, on "Hidden Mysteries," "Power of the Will," "Spiritual Growth," etc. The journalâ€™s prospectus called it "a serial publication devoted to advanced thought, giving special attention to questions bearing upon individual happiness, harmony, and health. An able exponent of the metaphysical principles of being." Offered with the Temple, May 1897, for the bargain price of $1.50 a year for both. At the time this was being published, Patterson was also editing Mind. NYPL.
