Bible Review.
Upholding Advanced Esoteric Thought.
1902-1923 Monthl, then bimonthly
Applegate, CA. Publisher: Esoteric Fraternity. Editor: Hiram Erastus Butler (1841-1916) until his death.
Succeeds: Occult and Biological Journal Succeeded by: Christian Esoteric
Corporate author: Esoteric Fraternity1/1 [15/1] October 1902-18/8 [32/8], June-July 1923. $1.00 a year. This continues The Esoteric and The Occult and Biological Journal. LOC.

Should be read in context with The Esoteric, The Occult and Biological Journal, and The Christian Esoteric, which are all Society Esoteric/Esoteric Fraternity publications.
