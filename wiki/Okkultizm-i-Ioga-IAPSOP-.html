    Periodical: Okkultizm i Ioga

Summary:  From Pat Deveney's database:

Okkulti'zm i ioga.
1933--1982?
Belgrade, Yugoslavia; Sofia, Bulgaria; Asunciun, Paraguay. Language: Russian. Publisher: Alexandr Mikhailovich Aseev. Editor: Alexandr Mikhailovich Aseev.
1/1, 1933-1982(?), but suspended 1938-1951, and then resumed in Sofia and Asunciun. 96-145 pp.

The journal was in its early days the semi-official organ of the Agni yoga work of Nicholas and Helena Roerich. Helena Roerich contributed material in her own name and as "T.P. Sudri." Described in Maria Carlson and R.H. Davis, Jr., "Russian Occult Journals and Newspapers," in Bernice Glatzer Rosenthan, The Occult in Russian and Soviet Culture (Ithaca: Cornell University Press, 1997). Stanford University; NYPL; Columbia University; University of Washington.
