 Almanach Astrologique.
 Magique, Prophetique, Satirique et des Sciences Occultes: Annuaire du Monde Elegant pour [1847] /  Scientifique, Astronomique, Physique, Satirique, Anecdotique, etc.  Magnetisme, electricite, Locomotion aerienne, Decouvertes nouvelles, Progr√®s, etc.
 Other titles: Almanach astrologique, scientifique, astronomique
 1847--1905  Annual
 Paris, France.  Language:  French.  Publisher: Bernard-Gustave Comon; Pagnerre; Depot central des almanachs.
  1/1, 1847-1905.  190 pp. (varies), 50 centimes an issue.  Although the almanac began in 1847, there seems to have been a lapse between that date and 1857 and none of the earlier issues survives.  This contained the usual features for almanacs: church and secular calendars, tables of moving feasts, seasons, eclipses, and appropriate times for various activities and predictions for the coming year.  Unlike its English contemporaries, the journal contained little instruction on astrology and its practice.  Interspersed through the journal were  articles on "The Tombs of the Jewish Patriarchs at Hebron," "The Price of Theater Seats," "Treatment of Hair Loss," and other filler.  Each issue had about 60 plates, initially by "Cham."  BNF; ZDB: Freiburg Inst Grenzgeb Psychol; Bamberg SB; Weimar HAAB.
