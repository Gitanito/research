 Sri Aurobindo Circle.
 1945--1998?  Annual
 Bombay, India.  Publisher: Sri Aurobindo Society.
 1/1, 1945.

This was an annual journal, unnumbered and undated except for year, of the students around Sri Aurobindo Ghose (1872-1950) and "The Mother" (Mirra Alfassa, 1878-1973), a former disciple of Alma and Max Theon and the Mouvement Cosmique.  Contributions by the Cambridge tutor and poet J.A. Chadwick ("Arjava") and his wife, Joyce, and by Gerald Heard.  University of Delaware; Syracuse University; Duke University.     Issues:Sri Aurobindo Circle N5 1949 Sri Aurobindo Circle N6 1950 Sri Aurobindo Circle N16 1960           </p>
