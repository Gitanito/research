 Revista de la Federacion Teosofica del Uruguay.
 1924?  Monthly
 Montevideo, Uruguay.  Succeeded by: Mensaje
 1/1, 1924 (?).  This was the standard Adyar Theosophical journal of the era, with translations of material from the Theosophist together with Spanish articles by the likes of Mario Roso de Luna and others and local news of activities in Uruguay.
