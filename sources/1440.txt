 Boletin de la Sociedad Teosofica en Uruguay.
 1935-1940?  Bimonthly, quarterly, annual, monthly
 Montevideo, Uruguay.  Language:  Spanish.
 1/1, December-January 1935-1936-August 1940.  This was a standard Adyar Theosophical Society journal, largely taken from the Theosophist, with an admixture of news of local activities.
