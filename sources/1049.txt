    Summary:&nbsp;  From Pat Deveney's database:

 Valoa Kohti ("Toward the Light").
 Teosofinen Kuukausijulkaisu ("Theosophical Monthly").
 Julkaisu Nykyajalle Ja Tulevalle ("Publishing for the Present and the Future")
 1917  Monthly, irregular
 New York, NY.  Language:  Finnish.
 Editor: Emil Kaarna.
  1/1, 1917.  
"At least 32 pages," $1.50 a year in the United States, $1.75 in Finland.

 The journal was intended as a link between Finnish-speaking Theosophists in America and as a way of spreading word of Theosophy there.  Kaarna had earlier edited Teosofian Valo in Finland.
