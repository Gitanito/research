  Jasenlehti ("Members Magazine").
 Suomen Teosofisen Seuran ("Finnish Theosophical Society").
 1918  Bimonthly
 Helsinki, Finland.  Language:  Finnish.
  1/1, January 1918. 
 16 pp., Smk. 2:50.

This was the product of an effort to revive the Theosophical Society and regularize its membership and activities.  It was intended to publish the minutes of the annual meetings, lodge notices and the like.     Issues:Jasenlehti V1 N1 Jan 1918 Jasenlehti V1 NS 4-5 Autumn 1918       </p>
