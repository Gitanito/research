 Psychic Research Quarterly, The.
 Other titles: Psyche, a quarterly review of psychology, in relation to education, psychoanalysis, industry, religion... & c.
 1920--1921  Quarterly, then annual from 1933
 London, England.  Publisher: Kegan, Paul, Trench, Trubner & Co. Ltd..  Editor: W. Whately Smith.  Succeeded by: Psyche: Psychic Research Quarterly
 1/1, July 1920-1/4, April 1921.  96 pp.

 Contributions by J. Arthur Hill, William Barrett, Eric J. Dingwall, et al.  With the second volume, July 1921, the journal became Psyche: Psychic Research Quarterly (q.v.)
