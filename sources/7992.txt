4737/21730/22088  					  				  			      			  			  				                                    Experience Description 4737  					</p>  					I had two heart attacks in 2007.  The first attack was in January when they put in two stents.  Then I had another heart attack in December of 2007.  That's when I had the out of body experience when I was on the operating table. I watched them take off my socks and my pants. Then I saw my wife (she has gray hair now) but when I saw her, she had black hair like she had when we met.  Then I saw the white light.  It was the brightest light I ever saw.

Gender:	 Male

Date NDE Occurred:	  12-04 2007
