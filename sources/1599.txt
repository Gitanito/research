 Fraternidad.
 Selecciones de Filosofia, Ciencia, Religion.  Hechos de la Vida y del Alma.  Ideas Nuevas -- Vida Nueva -- Mundo Nuevo.
 Other titles: Hacia la Fraternidad Universal
 1943  Monthly
 Santiago, Chile. 
 Language:  Spanish. 
 Publisher: Sociedad Teosofica de Chile. 
 Succeeded by: Hacia la Fraternidad Universal: Organo de la Sociedad Teosofica de Chile
  1/1, 1943.  20 pp.

 This was the organ of the the Theosophical Society of Chile.  The contents were dedicated "to those who think, to those who love, to those who suffer, and to those in doubt."  It contained articles by C. Jinarajadasa, Ernest Wood, and the usual post-war Theosophists, but also carried selections from Pearl Buck and Saint John of the Cross and an article on Bergson.  At some point before 1949 the journal changed its title to Hacia la Fraternidad Universal.
