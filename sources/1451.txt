 British Mazdaznan Magazine.
 The Official Organ of the Mazdaznan Association in Great Britain.
 1925-1938?  Monthly
 Manchester, then London, England.  Editor: Lt.-Col. Arthur F. Gault.
 1/1, 1925.  6/- a year, 48-68 pp.
