 This consisted primarily of Dowie's sermons, with each issue containing his photograph.  On him see the note under Leaves of Healing (Chicago). The journal carried regular advertisements for Dowie's vast conglomeration of profitable enterprises, including Zion College (with a Ministerial Training School, a Preparatory and Collegiate Department and a Junior School), Zion Land and Investment, and other businesses.  Occasional contributions by Mrs. (Jane) Dowie.  University of Texas; Newberry Library; NYPL, etc.
