 Flying Saucer News.
 The Official Journal of the Flying Saucer Club of Gt. Britain / Journal of the British Flying Saucer Bureau and FLying Saucer Club.
  Other titles: Flying Saucer News Bulletin
  1953--1955  Quarterly
  Hove, England. 
  Publisher: Bristol Flying Saucer Bureau. 
  Editor: Thomas M. Cornelia; Richard Hughes. 
  Succeeded by: Flying Saucer News Bulletin  Corporate author:  
 Flying Saucer Club (England)  1/1, Spring 1953-Spring 1955.  20 pp. (varies), 1/- a copy plus postage.

 The first seven issues were copies of typewritten pages.  The journal featured reports and  eye-witness accounts of UFOs around the world, book reviews and reports of investigations by the Flying Saucer Club and other clubs around Britain.  BL
