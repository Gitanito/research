 Ohio Spiritualist, The.
 Charity for all-Malice Toward None
 1868-1869  Weekly
 Cleveland, OH.  Publisher: H.O. Hammond.  Succeeded by: American Spiritualist (formed by merger of Ohio Spiritualist and Spiritualist of Janesville, OH)
 1/1, May 16, 1868-April 3, 1869.  $2.00 a year. 4 pp., 16 x 22.

The journal listed Ira Davenport as "State Agent."  Contributions by Hudson and Emma Tuttle.  The journal tried to walk a fine line between freedom and licentiousness, both denying that free-love abuses occurred and claiming that all right-thinking spiritualists condemned them.  The final issue noted: "With issue of April 17, 1869, vol. 2, no. 1, becomes The American Spiritualist.  NSAC Lily Dale; University of Rochester.
