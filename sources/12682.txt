Riley Probable NDE 22432  					  				  			    			  			  				  				  					Experience Description  					</p>  					I was hit by a car. The rest is really unspeakable. I was told not to tell, but I am.

Background Information:

Gender:	 Male

Date NDE Occurred:	  December 25 2010

  NDE Elements:

At the time of your experience, was there an associated life-threatening event?       Yes  Accident    Life threatening event, but not clinical death   I was hit by a car.

How do you consider the content of your experience?      Mixed

The experience included:      Out of body experience

Did you feel separated from your body?    Yes  I could see my own body in the streets.

At what time during the experience were you at your highest level of consciousness and alertness?       I was in pain.

Please compare your hearing during the experience to your everyday hearing that you had immediately prior to the time of the experience.    I heard dead people speaking as a legion of voices.

Did you pass into or through a tunnel?       No

The experience included:       Presence of deceased persons

Did you encounter or become aware of any deceased (or alive) beings?       Yes     I met dead relatives and other unknown beings.

The experience included:       Light

Did you see an unearthly light?       Yes   Bright light.

The experience included:       Strong emotional tone

What emotions did you feel during the experience?       A lot of emotions.

Did scenes from your past come back to you?       My past flashed before me, out of my control     I wasn't in control.  

God, Spiritual and Religion:

What was your religion prior to your experience?    Moderate   Christian

What is your religion now?       Liberal   Christian  

Concerning our Earthly lives other than Religion:     

After the NDE:

Did the questions asked and information that you provided accurately and comprehensively describe your experience?       Uncertain
