    Summary:&nbsp;  From Pat Deveney's database:

 Divine Science News, The.
 1943  Monthly
 Denver, CO.  Publisher: Colorado College of Divine Science.  Editor: Alice L. Moore.
 Succeeds: Fulfillment (1902-1906)-->Divine Science Quarterly (1906-1912)-->Power Magazine (1912?-1915)-->Aspire to Better Living (1916)-->Daily Studies in Divine Science (1916- 1935?)-->Divine Science Weekly (1919-1925)-->First Divine Science Church Weekly Bulletin (1928-1937)-->Divine Science Monthly (1935-1950)-->Aspire (1951-?)  Succeeded by: Divine Science Messenger (1976-1983?)
 1/1, September 1943.  8 1/2 x 11, 4-8 pp.  Free of charge.

This was a topical newsletter begun as a companion to The Divine Science Monthly which also was published by Nona L. Brooks' Divine Science College and First Divine Science Church in Denver.  The first issue was 4 pp., with a printing of 1, 500, and a year later it had expanded to 8 pp. with a printing of 6,600.  It probably began to decline with Brooks' death in March 1945.  Denver Public Library.       Issues:Divine Science News V1 N1 Sep 1944      </p>
