 Theosophy in India.
 The Gazette of the Indian Section of the Theosophical Society / Prasnottara.
 Other titles: Prasnottara
 1904–1927?  Monthly
 Benares, India.  Editor: George S. Arundale; General Secretary of the Theosophical Society in India.
 Succeeds: Prasnottara
 Corporate author:  Official Organ of the Theosophical Society, Indian Section
 1/1, January 1904-1927 (?).

 This was the continuation of Prasnottara, the organ of the Indian Section of the Theosophical Society.  It contained primarily notices of activities of the section and of activities in the Theosophical Society generally, together with book reviews and transcriptions of lectures by Annie Besant, C.W. Leadbeater, and others, but, especially in its early issues, carried articles on Indian occult topics.  ZDB: Tübingen UB; NYPL; Stanford University; National Library of Australia.
