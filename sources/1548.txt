 Eclectic Medical Journal.
 1849  Monthly
 Cincinnati, OH.  Publisher: Wm. White & Company.  Editor: Joseph Rodes Buchanan.
 1/1, January 1849 (which was 8/1 of the old series).  52 pp.

 Organ of the Eclectic Medical College of Cincinnati which had among its alumni Buchanan, John Ballou Newbrough, Caroline B. Winslow, Alice B. Stockham, John Uri Lloyd, and many other figures prominent in spiritualism, reform and mental healing.
