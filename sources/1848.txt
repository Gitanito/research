 Theosophy.
 A Magazine Devoted to the Theosophical Movement, the Brotherhood of Humanity, the Study of Occult Science and Philosophy and Aryan Literature.
 AUM
 1896-1897  Monthly
 New York, NY.  Editor: E.T. Hargrove, Katherine A. Tingley.
 Succeeds: The Path (1886-1896)  Succeeded by: Universal Brotherhood (1897-1899)->Universal Brotherhood Path (1900-1903) 11/1, April 1896-12/7, October 1897.

  With the issue of November 1897, the journal's name was changed to Universal Brotherhood.  32-64 pp.  Published by W.Q. Judge's disciples after his death, including J.D. Buck, Franz Hartmann, James M. Pryse, J.H. Fussell, Alfred E.S. Smythe, E.A. Neresheimer, Charles and Vera Johnston, "Jasper Niemand," Alice L. Cleather, Elliott B. Page, Basil Crump, Katherine Tingley, Alexander Wilder, Albert E.S. Smythe, James M. Pryse, et al., most of whom followed Katherine A. Tingley.  Regular "Mirror of the Movement" with the activities of the Theosophical Society in America.  Indexed  by the Campbell Theosophical Research Library, Sydney, Australia, online at http://www.austheos.org.  Reprinted Edmonton, Canada: Edmonton Theosophical Society, n.d.
