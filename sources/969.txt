 Twin City Spiritualist News, The.
 A Monthly Magazine Devoted to Spiritualism and its Workers.
 Truth is not worth having without, if need be, a man will die for it - Bruno / We ask the Thinkers to Think
 1912?  Monthly
 Minneapolis, MN.  Editor: Rev. J.F. Ireland.  Corporate author:  First Spiritualist Church of Minneapolis
  1/1, 1912 (?)  18 pp, 5 cents a copy.

 Ireland was the president of the Mississippi Valley Spiritualist Association in Clinton, Iowa, and pastor of the First Spiritualist Church in Minneapolis.  The journal seems to have been the vehicle for publication of Ireland's sermons and for advertisements for favored spiritualist churches and mediums.  One issue at NSAC, Lily Dale; Hamilton College.      Issues:Twin City Spiritualist News March 1912        </p>
