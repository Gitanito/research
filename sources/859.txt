 Spiritualist, The.
 The Organ of the Spiritualist Community.
 1930  Monthly
 London, England.  Publisher: Golden Vista Press.
  1/1, November 1930.  32 pp., 3d. an issue, 4s. a year.

 The journal contained contributions by Arthur Ford, musings on idealism, brotherhood, the spiritualizing of spiritualism, spiritual awakening, congregational singing, and book reviews, and the first (and possibly the last) issue contained a supplement with "Li-La, A Romance of the East."  It was published from Grotrian Hall in London and devoted extensive space to musical performances and lectures given there.  University of Texas, Austin.
