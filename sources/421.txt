 Archiv fur den Thierischen Magnetismus.
 1804--1808  Irregular
 Jena, Germany.  Language:  German.
 Editor: August W. Nordhoff.
 Publisher: Hofbuchdrucker Gopferdts.
  1/1, 1804.

 Two issues appeared in Jena in 1804 and catalogues state that a later volume was published in 1808.  Henry Carrington Bolton, A Catalogue of Scientific and Technical Periodicals, 1665-1882 (Washington, 1885), 72.  Bayerische Staatsbibliothek.
