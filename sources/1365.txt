 Annales Theosophiques.
 1908-1916?  Quarterly
 Paris, France.  Language:  French.  Editor: Gaston Revel, founder and editor.
 1/1, March 1909-1916(?)

 The last surviving issue is April-June 1914, but the journal was still being advertised in Lotus Bleu in August 1916.  Gaston Revel founded and edited this and other small Theosophical journals, and also edited La Revue Theosophique / Le Lotus Bleu from 1914 to 1919.  BNF; Societe Theosophique de France.
