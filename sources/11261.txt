NDE 6126  					  				  			        			  			  				  				  					Experience Description  					</p>  					I was in the room, but not in the bed.  I saw an intense white light and heard a pleasant classical music associated with the light, surrounded by an agreeable atmosphere.  It was a pleasant experience.  Afterwards, I felt that I did not fear death anymore (though that fear returned later as my cancer had not been cured).

Background Information:

Gender:	 Female

Date NDE Occurred:	  2010-10-25

  NDE Elements:
