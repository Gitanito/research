 Boletin del Centro de Estudios Psicologicos.
 1930
 Sabadell, Spain.  Language:  Spanish.  Editor: Ramon Casanova.
 1/1, April 1930.  14 pp., distributed without charge.

 The journal was intended to serve to introduce readers to all the activities of the Centro, including lectures on "Las modernas orientaciones de la economia social," "Materialismo y Espiritualismo," "Los siete senderos de evolucion," "El Naturismo cientifico como medio de regeneracion humana," etc., all of which centered around naturism, vegetarianism, and artistic matters.  Noted in  Oscar M. Garcia Rodriguez, "Hemerografia Espiritista Espaola, 1857-1936," https://grupoespiritaisladelapalma.wordpress.com.  BNE.
