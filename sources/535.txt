    Summary&nbsp;  From Pat Deveney's database:

 Magnetiseur, Le.
 Journal du Magnetisme Animal.
 1859-1872  Monthly
 Geneva, Switzerland.  Language:  French.  Editor: Charles Lafontaine.
 1/1, April 15, 1859-1872.  16 pp., 5-10 francs, depending on destination.

 As its name indicates, the journal was primarily devoted to magnetism, but it was also one of the forums for the discussions over spiritualism and the postulated causes of its effects.  Crabtree 807.  Universitat Zurich.  Google Books.
