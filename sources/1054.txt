 Urania.
 A Monthly Journal of Astrology, Metereology and Physical Science.
 1880--1880  Monthly
 London, England.  Publisher: Simpkin, Marshall & Co..  Editor: Alfred J. Pearce.
  1/1-9, January-September 1880.  6s. 6d. a year.  32 pp.  9 issues only.

 Pearce (1849-1923) was a homeopathic physician and belonged to the "scientific" side of astrology, as contrasted with the occult side.  He also edited Star Lore, the Future and Zadkiel's Almanac.  On him see the note under Star Lore.  Advertised in Banner of Light 47/19 (July 31, 1880): 5.  Northwestern University.
