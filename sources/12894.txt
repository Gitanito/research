  		              		Experience Description   

    I got a flu shot before and passed out in the waiting room. 

I remember passing out of my body,  through a window with a lot of light, and looking down on what was happening.  I then remember returning back to the table.   

   I don't know how I got back to the examining table.  I felt very weak after awakening and rested for a while then left of my own power and drove home  					  

Background Information:       

Gender:   	Male  

Date NDE Occurred:   	 Early 1970s  

NDE Elements:       

  At the time of your experience, was there an associated life-threatening event?        Yes    Allergic Reaction       Other unsure of exact definition passed out for several minutes.  I was given a flu shot... felt a buzz in my head in the doctor's waiting room ... woke up in a sweat with two doctors standing over me with iv... not sure of the exact length of time or how I got back into the doctors examining room  

How do you consider the content of your experience?        Both pleasant AND distressing  

The experience included:      Out of body experience  

Did you feel separated from your body?      No    I clearly left my body and existed outside it  

  How did your highest level of consciousness and alertness during the experience compare to your normal everyday consciousness and alertness?        More consciousness and alertness than normal.   More focused because of the centralization of my view of me on the table  

At what time during the experience were you at your highest level of consciousness and alertness?         I don't know how to answer this question  

Were your thoughts speeded up?         No  

Did time seem to speed up or slow down?         No     

Were your senses More vivid than usual?         More vivid than usual  

Please compare your vision during the experience to your everyday vision that you had immediately prior to the time of the experience.       Much more focused... I am generally a person who takes into account all of my surroundings.  During the experience I was focused on me on the table.  

Please compare your hearing during the experience to your everyday hearing that you had immediately prior to the time of the experience.      I don't remember hearing anything specific  

Did you seem to be aware of things going on elsewhere, as if by ESP?       No  

The experience included:       Passing into or through a tunnel  

  Did you pass into or through a tunnel?         Uncertain     I followed a light beam path. Not sure if it was a tunnel of light  

Did you see any beings in your experience?         No  

Did you encounter or become aware of any deceased (or alive) beings?         No       

Did you see, or feel surrounded by, a brilliant light?         An unusually bright light  

Did you see an Unearthly light?         Yes   a tunnel of light bringing me both away from and back toward the table  

Did you seem to enter some other, unearthly world?         No       

What emotions did you feel during the experience?         Relaxed ... what was going to occur was going to occur  

Did you have a feeling of peace or pleasantness?         No  

Did you have a feeling of joy?         No  

Did you feel a sense of harmony or unity with the universe?       No  

Did you suddenly seem to understand everything?         No       

Did scenes from your past come back to you?         No       

Did scenes from the future come to you?         No       

Did you come to a border or point of no return?         No

After the NDE:       

Was the experience difficult to express in words?        No     

  How accurately do you remember the experience in comparison to other life events that occurred around the time of the experience?       I remember the experience more accurately than other life events that occurred around the time of the experience.  I remember the event, yet I don't remember what I ate that day
