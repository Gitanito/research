 Theosophical News.
 A Weekly Report of Activities.
 1896—1897  Weekly

 Boston, Mount Vernon, MA.  Publisher: Boston Theosophical News Publication. Succeeds: New England Notes  Succeeded by: New Century (1897-1903); Merged with Universal Brotherhood Path to form New Century Path (1903-1911)—>Theosophical Path (1911-1935)
  1/1, June 22, 1896-2/14, September 20, 1897.  4 pp., 11 x 14, $1.00 a year.

  Noted in Pacific Theosophist, 1896.  The journal adhered to the Katherine Tingley branch of Theosophy and regularly carried news of her her crusades and speeches.  Plates of Tingley, E.T. Hargrove, E. August Neresheimer, Robert Crosbie, Constance Hargrove, Alice L. Cleather, Herbert Crooke,  D.N. Dunlop, et al.  Rowell’s American Newspaper Directory for 1900 says the journal’s circulation was less than 1,000.  ATLA microfilm; Boston University; United Library (Illinois).
