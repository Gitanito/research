 Almanach de la Survie pour l'annee [1900].
 1900  Annual
 Paris, France.  Publisher: Chamuel.  Editor: Albin Valabregue.
 1/1, 1900.  74 pp.

  The almanac was said to have been produced to expound a scientific form of spiritualism in contradistinction to what was said to be the prevailing sectarianism.   Includes a discourse by the spirit of Allen Kardec and a play illustrative of the beauties of socialism.  BNF
