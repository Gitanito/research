 American Journal of Eugenics.
 1907-1910
 Chicago, IL, then Los Angeles, CA.  Editor: Moses Harman.
 Succeeds: Lucifer
 1907-January-February 1910.

 This was the last incarnation of Moses Harman's Lucifer the Lightbearer after he finished his final imprisonment in 1907 and moved toward a more scholarly presentation of individual sovereignty, sexology, women's rights, freedom of speech,and especially racial uplift and progress through "eugenics."  Contributions by Theodore Schroeder, E.B. Foote, et al.
