 National Messenger, The.
 1895
 Washington, D.C.  Editor: H.D. Barrett.
 Corporate author:  National Spiritualist Association
1/1, June 1895

  This was the organ of the National Spiritualist Association, begun in 1893,  the predecessor of the National Spiritualist Association of Churches.   It was devoted to furthering the position of the NSA in the then on-going conflict among spiritualists over the wisdom of a forming a national organization.  NSAC Lily Dale.
