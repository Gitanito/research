     Summary:&nbsp;  From Pat Deveney's database:

 My Messenger.
 Words from The Master, offered in all humility from those who received them.
 1935  Monthly
 Binfield, England(?). 
 Publisher: Printed for the Circle of Blessing.
 Corporate author:  Circle of Blessing
  1/1, 1935.

  The issue for December 1935 has a handwritten message on its cover from Jesus, the Christ of God, “wishing and hoping” that readers would “closely follow in the footsteps of your worthy Father.”  University of Texas, Austin (nos. 7-11, 1935, in Arthur Conan Doyle’s papers).
