Describes parapsychological research involving animals, from relatively unsophisticated testing in the 1930s to the well-designed laboratory work of today.                                           Experimental Psi Research in Asia and Australia                

Overview of the history and relative contributions in the field of psi research by labs and individuals in Australia, Japan, China and India                                           Cleve Backster                

Cleve Backster (1924–2013),&nbsp;a polygraph expert, carried out experiments which he claimed showed a degree of conscious awareness in plants.&nbsp;                                           Blackmore-Sargent Controversy – A Reconsideration                

A new analysis&nbsp;of accusations of cheating made by Susan Blackmore against Carl Sargent, a fellow psi experimenter active in the 1970s, finds breaches of protocol but no&nbsp;deliberate fraud.                                           CD Broad                

Cambridge philosopher&nbsp;(1887–1971) whose interests encompassed parapsychology, and who&nbsp;theorized on topics such as precognition and post-mortem survival.                                           Psi Research in China                

This article describes some recent experimental and theoretical work in China, including successful attempts to train ESP abilities in blind children. It has been contributed by Professor Yi-Fang Chang, a physicist at China’s Yumman University.                                           Creative Subjects in Ganzfeld                

Series of successful ESP experiments with&nbsp;dance, drama and music students of New York’s Juilliard School, who scored at twice the rate of subjects drawn from the general population.&nbsp;                                           Creery Telepathy Experiments                

Successful early experiments in telepathy (1882-1888)&nbsp;carried out by SPR researchers with a family of five girls in the north of England, which were eventually somewhat discredited by revelations of cheating.&nbsp;&nbsp;                                           Delgado-Romero and Howard Telepathy Experiments                

American psychologists Edward Delgado-Romero and George Howard carried out controversial ganzfeld telepathy experiments in 2005, with a view to correcting ‘flawed’ parapsychological findings.                                           Dermo-Optical Perception
