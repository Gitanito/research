 This was a typical Adyar Theosophical journal of the period, with articles by Annie Besant, C.W. Leadbeater, C. Jinarajadasa but few references to H.P. Blavatsky.  It also carried articles on Alexandrian Neoplatonism and other subjects concordant with the unusual inclusion of Psiquicas and Espiritualistas in the journal's subtitle.  Casa Pessoa, Lisbon.
