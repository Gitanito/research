Nigel Buckmaster Contributors                                                                                                                           Other Phenomena                                                                                                                                                          Crop Circles                

The intricate patterns that sometimes appear spontaneously in crop fields have been attributed variously to human hand, freak weather phenomena, ley lines, contacts with spirits, and visitations by extra-terrestrials.                                           De Rochas’ s L&#039;Extériorisation de la Motricité                 

Book published in 1896 by French researcher Albert de Rochas, L'Extériorisation de la Motricité,&nbsp;giving&nbsp;detailed description of physical séance phenomena produced by Eusapia Palladino and others.                                           Doris Fischer
