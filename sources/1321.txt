 Monthly Letter, A.
 Devoted to Spiritual and Philosophical Problems.
 Other titles: Student’s Monthly Letter / Carta Mensual
 1934--1941?  Monthly
 Los Angeles, CA.  Language:  English and Spanish.  Publisher: Phoenix Press.  Editor: Manly Palmer Hall.

 1/1, July 1, 1934-1941(?).  4 pp.  The letter initially accompanied a lesson or talk by Hall, usually 12-pages in length, on topics like "Definitions of Philosophy" and "Some Philosophical Fragments."  With May 1937 the title of the journal was changed to Student’s Monthly Letter, and then in 1940 was changed back to the initial title.  A Spanish version (Carta Mensuel) was published for a time beginning in 1935.
