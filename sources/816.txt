 	Spiritual News, The.
 A Record of the Progress of Spiritualism in Great Britain.
 1870–1871  Monthly
 London, England.  Editor: William Henry Harrison.  Succeeded by: Spiritualist / Spiritualist Newspaper

 1/1-5, December 1870-April 1871.  1 d., 4 pp.  Harrison started this to ease the space pressure on the Spiritualist and to supply a spiritual journal at a penny a copy.  The fifth issue announced the journal’s suspension but reserved the right to the name if the situation improved.  Harry Price Library, University of London; BL.
