Nigel Buckmaster Contributors                                                                                                                           Robert McLuhan                                                                                                                                           

Robert McLuhan studied English Literature at Oxford, then worked as a foreign correspondent for the&nbsp;Guardian&nbsp;in Spain and Portugal. He now works as a freelance journalist. He has been a member of the Society for Psychical&nbsp;Research since 1993, blogging and speaking&nbsp;on paranormal topics. His 2010 book&nbsp;Randi’s Prize&nbsp;is a critique of sceptical arguments against psychical research. In 2014, he was appointed to run the SPR’s Buckmaster Publications Projects, which include the creation of the Psi Encyclopedia.
