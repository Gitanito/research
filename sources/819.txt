 International Theosophist, The.
 A Monthly Magazine Devoted to the Furtherance of Universal Brotherhood Under the Leadership of Katherine A. Tingley.
 1898--1904  Monthly
 Dublin, Ireland, London, England, New York, NY.  Publisher: International Theosophical Press (Dublin).  Editor: H.A.W. Coryn and Frederick John Dick.

 Succeeds: The Theosophic Isis (1896-February 1897)-->The Grain (March 1897-August-September 1897)-->merged with The Irish Theosophist (October 1897-March 1898) to form The Internationalist (October 1897-March 1898)  1/1, April 1898-6/9, September 1904.  20 pp., 4s. a year.

  Journal devoted to the Katherine A. Tingley branch of Theosophy.  Contributions by or excerpts from W.Q. Judge, D.N. Dunlop, Alice L. Cleather, John Eglinton.  BL; Harvard University; Indiana University; University of Kansas; University of Texas; Brigham Young University.
