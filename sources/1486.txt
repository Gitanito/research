 Christian Science Weekly / Sentinel.
 To Hold Guard over Truth, Life, and Love
 Other titles: Christian Science Sentinel
 1898  Weekly
 Boston, MA.  Publisher: Christian Science Publishing Society.  Editor: Mary Baker Eddy; Septimus J. Hanna, C.S.D., editor, Camilla Hanna, C.S.D., assistant editor.

 1/1, 1898.  $1.00 a year, 24 pp.  The journal was started as the Christian Science Weekly and changed its name to Christian Science Sentinel in February 1899.  It was originally founded by Mary Baker Eddy primarily to spread accounts of healings from a Christian Science view.  LOC.
