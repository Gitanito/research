 Ruusu-Risti [Rose-Cross].
 Okkultinen Aikakauskirja [Occult Magazine].
 1905?--1939?  Monthly
 Helsinki, Finland.
  Language:  Finnish.
 Editor: Pekka Ervast; Eino Krohn.
  1/1, 1905 (?)  (The seventeenth volume is dated 1921.)

 Pekka Ervast, the editor, was a prominent Theosophist but the journal was devoted to occultism generally.  It written largely by Finnish authors, although it carried excerpts from the writings of H.P. Blavatsky, Bo Yin Ra, Dion Fortune, P.G. Bowen, and H. Spencer Lewis.
