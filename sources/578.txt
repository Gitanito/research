 Raja-Yoga Messenger.
 An Illustrated Magazine/Monthly  devoted to the Higher Education of Youth.
 1904-1929  Monthly, then bimonthly, then quarterly
 Point Loma, CA.  Editor: Katherine TIngley, Conducted by Students of the Raja Yoga College.  Succeeded by: Lotus Circle Messenger
 1/1, January 1904-1929.  16pp., 50 cents a year.

 A children’s magazine that is actually a paean to the wonders and joys of Lomaland and its Raja Yoga Academy/School, replete with illustrations of the property and of its inhabitants (acting in ways deemed suitable by Tingley), and with extended lessons in and articles on history, art, literature,  and dance from the students’ instructions.  Contributions (in 1924) by Talbot Mundy.  Noted in Hartmann’s Who’s Who, 1925.  University of Illinois; Point Loma Nazarene University; Bibliotheek van de Universiteit van Amsterdam,;Oxford University; NYPL; Wilmington University; University of Massachusetts, Amherst; McGill University.
