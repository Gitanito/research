					Heather T NDE  					  					  						Home  						Classification NDE  						5218 Heather T NDE 9662  					  				  			      			  			  				

  		              		Experience Description   

    I remember seeing my mom and dad at my side. They were crying and holding my hands as the doctors came in. The doctors were there to provide medical assistance and get my heart pumping again. After the doctors came in. I remember turning around and I seeing a bright light  behind me. The only thing I remember afterwards is waking up in my hospital room.  
  
Background Information:     

Gender: 	Female  

Date NDE Occurred: 	 1980  

NDE Elements:     

At the time of your experience, was there an associated life-threatening event?       Yes    Surgery-related       Clinical death (cessation of breathing or heart function)   I had just got out of open heat surgery and was in recovery  

How do you consider the content of your experience?      Neither pleasant NOR distressing  

Did you feel separated from your body?    Yes  I heard the conversations between my parents and the doctors,  I just don't recall what was said now.  I clearly left my body and existed outside it  

How did your highest level of consciousness and alertness during the experience compare to your normal everyday consciousness and alertness?      Don't recall  

At what time during the experience were you at your highest level of consciousness and alertness?       Don't recall  

Were your thoughts speeded up?       No  

Did time seem to speed up or slow down?       No     

Were your senses More vivid than usual?       No  

Please compare your vision during the experience to your everyday vision that you had immediately prior to the time of the experience.     Don't recall  

Please compare your hearing during the experience to your everyday hearing that you had immediately prior to the time of the experience.    Don't recall  

Did you seem to be aware of things going on elsewhere?     No  

Did you pass into or through a tunnel?       No       

Did you see any beings in your experience?       No  

Did you encounter or become aware of any deceased (or alive) beings?       No       

Did you see, or feel surrounded by, a brilliant light?       An unusually bright light  

Did you see an unearthly light?       Yes   I looked behind me and saw a bright light and walked towards it.  

Did you seem to enter some other, unearthly world?       No       

What emotions did you feel during the experience?       I was sad that my parents were crying and sad and scared  

Did you have a feeling of peace or pleasantness?       No  

Did you have a feeling of joy?       No

Did you suddenly seem to understand everything?       No       

Did scenes from your past come back to you?       No

After the NDE:     

Was the experience difficult to express in words?      No     

How accurately do you remember the experience in comparison to other life events that occurred around the time of the experience?     I remember the experience more accurately than other life events that occurred around the time of the experience  I don't remember much of my childhood,  but I recall that moment  

Do you have any psychic, non-ordinary or other special gifts after your experience that you did not have before the experience?       No
