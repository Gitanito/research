 Tour Saint-Jacques, La.
 1955--1958  Bimonthly
 Paris, France.  Language:  French.  Publisher: Tour Saint-Jacques.  Editor: Robert Amadou.  Succeeded by: Cahiers de la Tour Saint-Jacques, Les
 1/1, November-December 1955-July-August 1958.  152-212 pp.

  This has scholarly and interesting articles on or by Louis Massignon, Sri Auribindo, Nicholas Flamel (by Canseliet), Rene Guenon, et al., and on a variety of alchemical, paranormal and occult subjects.  It included as a supplement Bulletin de parapsychologie (1955-1958).        Issues:Tour Saint Jacques  N13-14  Jan-apr 1958      </p>
