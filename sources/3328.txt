 O.T.O. Newsletter.
 Do what thou wilt shall be the whole of the Law.  -- Liber Al: 1, 40.
 1977--1983  Quarterly, irregular.
 Berkeley, CA. .
 Editor: Caliph Hymenaeus Alpha (Grady Louis McMurtry, 1918-1985); editor and printer Bill Heidrick. .
 Succeeded by: The Magical Link.
  1/1, May 1977-1983.

 This was the newsletter of McMurtry's Califate, reproduced from typed 8 1/2 x 11 sheets and stapled.  10-120 pp., $3.50 a year in North America, $5.00 international.   The journal lapsed in 1980 and was then re-begun by McMurtry in 1982.  The newsletter prominently reproduced McMurtry's authorization from Aleister Crowley to "take charge of the whole work of the Order in California."  The journal contained work by Crowley and others.  University of London, Warburg.
