Hello there.
  	
  	My name is Giovanni. I have been studying NDE's for about 8 months now and   	am currently reading Dr. Longs book &quot;Evidence of the Afterlife&quot; as well as   	Dr. Pim van Lommel M.D.'s book &quot;Consciousness Beyond Life&quot; , both very   	intriguing, informative, and best of all scientifically oriented in the   	presentation of the facts.
  	
  	Being a science minded person myself, I have accumulated NDE data from video   	documentaries in order to organize data to find commonalities between NDE's   	and figure out why certain experiences happen to certain people. I only have   	50-some cases that don't have the variety or quantity of data you have on   	NDERF but have organized the data in a table (attached) that allow for   	studies to be conducted in a customizable way respective to 23 NDE elements   	I have taken interest in. For example if someone wanted to do research on   	people experiencing positive / negative experiences in relation to their   	prior existing cultural beliefs, the table could be used to study those   	relationships as well as other factors that pertain to that particular NDE   	experience.
  	
  	I am not trying to make money off this or have a catch of any kind. I just   	felt inspired to share what I have done in the hope to further the cause of   	clarifying the reality of afterlife, that I myself have (through scientific   	means) convinced myself is a reality.
  	
  	God bless!
  	
  	Giovanni
