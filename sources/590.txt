 Succeeds: Reflexionen aus der Geisterwelt  Corporate author:  Verein spiriter Forscher
  1/1, January 1878-1899(?)  Four Austrian florins or 7 Reichsmark a year, 4 pp.

  Replaced Reflexionen aus der Geisterwelt published in Budapest by the same society, and like that journal it carried the articles and trance revelations of Adelma von Vay and long dissertations explicating each of the Gospels and topics such as love, ethics, etc.  The society was presided over by Adolf Grunhut and was decidedly Kardecist in its approach, and the journal reflects that viewpoint.  Hungarian National Library, Budapest;  SBB; Osterreichische Nationalbibliothek.
