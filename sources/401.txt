 Annales du Magnetisme Animal.
 1814-1816  Irregular
 Paris, France.  Language:  French.  Publisher: J.G. Dentu.  Succeeded by: Bibliotheque du Magnetisme Animal->Archives du Magnetisme Animal
 Corporate author:  Societe du Magnetisme Animal
1/1, July 1814-1816.  The journal appeared in fascicles of about 48 pp.  It was an early and important journal of mesmerism, with reports by Puysegur, articles on the history of animal magnetism before Mesmer,  theoretical articles on the classification of "crises," and numerous reports of treatments.  Crabtree 248.
