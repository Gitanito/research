    Summary:&nbsp;  Light and Life.
 An Unsectarian Magazine of Mystic Literature.
 1886  Monthly
 Glasgow, Scotland.  Publisher: Light and Life.
 1/1, marked “new series,” August 1886.  16 pp, 2s./issue, $1.00 to US.

  The journal contains occasional references to spiritualism and occultism (including regular notices of R.F. Fryar's offerings and articles by Thomas Moore Johnson, a Platonist and member of the H.B. of L.), but was devoted mainly to studies of Boehme, Pordage, Madame Guyon, et al., and to alchemy, Quakerism, etc.  Hamilton College, Harvard, U of Glasgow.
