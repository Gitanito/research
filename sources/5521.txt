 1897-1900  Monthly
 New York, NY.  Publisher: Alliance Publishing Company.  Editor: Charles Brodie Patterson.
 1/1, May 1897-April 1900. 16 pp.,  $1.00 a year.  The journal was a platitudinous series of ruminations by Patterson, on "Hidden Mysteries," "Power of the Will," "Spiritual Growth," etc.  The journalâ€™s prospectus called it "a serial publication devoted to advanced thought, giving special attention to questions bearing upon individual happiness, harmony, and health.
