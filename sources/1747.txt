  Spiritual Science Magazine.
 The New Philosophy.
 1934--1939?  Monthly
 Los Angeles, CA.
 Editor: Gordon P. Hayne.
 Publisher: National Federation of Spiritual Science Churches.
  1/1, March 1934. 
 $1.00 a year, 24 pp.

  This was the organ of the National Federation of Spiritual Sciences Churches, a primarily West Coast organization, founded in 1927 in Los Angeles. The organization advocated a Christian form of spiritualism ("Man is a Creation of GOD and an inheritor of all His Divine Attributes, and is destined to eternal progression . . .") and practiced spirit healing and "soul unfoldment."  The journal carried advertisements for Aluminum Trumpets (standard size, three sections, light weight, $3.00).  University of California, Berkeley.
