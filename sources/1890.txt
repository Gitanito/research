 Yhteis-Vapaamuurari [Co-Freemason].
 1921--1926
 Helsinki, Finland. 
 Language:  Finnish.
 Editor: Kullikki Ignatius, R. Candolin, Jussi Snellman, Laina Brummert, Edwin Larsson.
 Succeeds: Spun off by Ruusu-Risti in 1921 
 Succeeded by: Kulmamitta
  1/1, October 1921-1926.

 This was a journal primarily devoted to Le Droit Humain Masonry.  It had been part of the more generally occult Ruusu-Risti magazine and then spun off.  Contributions by or excerpts from Oswald Wirth.
