 Sendero, El.
 Publicacion mensual de la Sociedad Cultural "Amado Nervo".
 1931  Monthly
 Teziutlan, Mexico.  Language:  Spanish.  Editor: Ruben Alanis Rucol.
 1/1, June 1931.  4 pp.
