 Licht en Waarheid.
 Panfilosofisch Tijdschrift.
 Wie Denkt Overwint.  Urijheid door broederschap.  De mensch is godd'lijk!  In hem woont gewis een God, het schoole beeld der godheid zelve.  Wijk dus geen schrede van uw mench-zijn!
 1890--1926?  Weekly, semimonthly, monthly
 Amsterdam, Netherlands.
  Language:  Dutch. 
 Corporate author:  Orgaan van de vereeniging Wie Denkt Overwint  1/1, 1890.  4-16 pp., f. 2.65 a year.

 This was the organ of the "Wie Denkt Overwint" ("The one who thinks, wins") association, and was one of the journals like Modern Mystics, Mensch en Kosmos, the Eranos Yearbooks, etc., that sought to synthesize all religious, spiritualist and occult thought into a rational mode of life in opposition to materialism.  See the note under Mensch en Kosmos.  Unlike those journals, this was more strongly Christian in tone.  Its emblem was the Tao shedding rays of light.  It presented excerpts from various Theosophical and occult journals and short articles on and about Bulwer Lytton, Johannes Trithemius, Jacob Boehme, Apollonius of Tyana, Melchi-zedek, et al.  Notable articles on the Rosicrucians by "Fr. Syntheticus" (the Theosophist H.J. van Ginkel, who later edited De Ster in het Oosten).  Delpher.nl/nl.
