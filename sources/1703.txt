 Ihminen [Man].
 A Monthly Publication Dedicated to Philosophy, Religion, Science.
 1922  Monthly
 Helsinki, Finland.
 Editor: G.E.O. Knight, honorary editor; Maunu Teraskivi.
 Succeeded by: Yhteis-Vapaamuurari (offshoot)
  1/1, November 1922-1926.

 This was a popular journal devoted to Masonry, Theosophy, the Rosy Cross and the occult generally, with the stated goal of contributing to the "cultivation of the spiritual cultivation of our nation by plowing its furrow in accordance" with its programÂ -- "Live and act in such a way that you contribute to all that unites us humans, because isolation is an illusion.  Therefore be a brother to all."  Short articles on Mecca, the Dalai Lama, Stonehenge, etc., and regular contributions by Charles Lazenby.
