161  					  				  			      			  			  				  				  					Experience Description  					</p>  					I had just started the fifteenth seizure when everything went black. The next thing that happened I found myself walking barefoot but I had been wearing hiking boots in midwinter. The rest is very personal and private.

Background Information:

Gender:	 Male

Date NDE Occurred:	  Sept 1985

  NDE Elements:

At the time of your experience, was there an associated life-threatening event?       Yes    Epilepsy attack   multiple myoclonic seizures  Multiple seizures.

How do you consider the content of your experience?      Mixed

Did you feel separated from your body?    Yes

At what time during the experience were you at your highest level of consciousness and alertness?       Learned more out of my body than in it.

Please compare your hearing during the experience to your everyday hearing that you had immediately prior to the time of the experience.    I was told all is well by God the Son himself.

Did you pass into or through a tunnel?       No

The experience included:       Presence of deceased persons

Did you encounter or become aware of any deceased (or alive) beings?       Yes     My grandmother but I did not speak to her.

The experience included:       Darkness

Did you see an unearthly light?       No

The experience included:       A landscape or city

What emotions did you feel during the experience?       Joy.

Did you suddenly seem to understand everything?       Everything about the universe     I have a telepathic connection with people in another universe not our own.

Did scenes from the future come to you?       Scenes from the world's future

Did you come to a border or point of no return?       No   I didn't want to come back I wanted to stay.  

God, Spiritual and Religion:

What was your religion prior to your experience?    Conservative/fundamentalist

Did you have a change in your values and beliefs because of your experience?       Yes     I do not care to be here at all.  

     After the NDE:

Was the experience difficult to express in words?      Yes   I was ordered to return and I didn't want to! I wanted to stay, for the first time in my life, I was happy. I haven't been so since and nothing has changed this fact.

Do you have any psychic, non-ordinary or other special gifts after your experience that you did not have before the experience?       Yes     Extreme range telepathy.

Are there one or several parts of your experience that are especially meaningful or significant to you?        Coming back.

Did the questions asked and information that you provided accurately and comprehensively describe your experience?       Uncertain
