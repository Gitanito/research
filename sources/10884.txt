I was having fainting spells.

Background Information:

Gender:    Female

Date NDE Occurred:    'August, 1992'

NDE Elements:

At the time of your experience, was there an associated life-threatening event?      Yes   Illness    'Life threatening event, but not clinical death' Arrhythmia.

How do you consider the content of your experience?      Positive

The experience included:      Out of body experience

Did you feel separated from your body?    Yes.  I, my spirit, was golden light.  I was still attached from the center of my chest.  My spirit appeared purple at the junction with my body.

At what time during the experience were you at your highest level of consciousness and alertness?      I was very alert.

Did your hearing differ in any way from normal?    The doctors in the background trying to bring me back.

Did you pass into or through a tunnel?      No

The experience included:      Light

Did you see an unearthly light?      Yes   A beautiful, golden light.

Did you seem to enter some other, unearthly world?     A clearly mystical or unearthly realm     I was very aware of my senses before, during, and after the experience.

The experience included:      Strong emotional tone

What emotions did you feel during the experience?      I felt awe at God's love for me.

Did you come to a border or point of no return?      No

God, Spiritual and Religion:

Did you have a change in your values and beliefs because of your experience?      Yes     I have stronger beliefs.

After the NDE:

Was the experience difficult to express in words?     No

Do you have any psychic, non-ordinary or other special gifts after your experience that you did not have before the experience?      Yes     My voice was immensely improved.  I sometimes have extra sensory perception.

At any time in your life, has anything ever reproduced any part of the experience?    No
