 Astrologer, The.
 1950  Monthly
 Springfield, MA.  
Publisher: Harle Publications, Inc.
 Editor: Grant Lewi. Succeeds: Today’s Astrology (combines)
  1/1, 1950.

 This was the work of William Grant Lewi (1902-1951, a one-time professor of English at Columbia University, Dartmouth College and other schools who then, in the 1930s took up astrology.  He was the author of two of the most influential books on the subject: Heaven Knows What and Astrology for the Millions.  Lewi edited Dell’s Horoscope in the 1930s and 1940s.  National Library of Scotland; University of Illinoinois at Urbana Champaign.
