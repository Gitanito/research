 Annee Occultiste et Psychique, L'.
 Ou expose annuel des observations scientifiques et des travaux publies en France et Ã  l'Etranger dans les sciences mysterieuses Astrologie, Alchimie, Symbolique, Esoterisme, arts divinatoires, Prophetique, psychisme, spiritisme, Magnetisme.
 1908--1909
 Paris, France.  Language:  French.  Publisher: Henri Daragon.  Editor: Pierre Piobb (Pierre Vincenti Comte da Piobetta).
  1/1, 1908 (for the year 1907)-1909 (for 1908).

  "Piobb" (Comte Pierre Vincenti, 1874-1942) was a Corsican journalist and a well-respected astrologer, magician and occultist.  He wrote on, among other things, Les Mysteres des Dieux: Venus, la Deesse Magique de la Chair, and a book on the secret of Nostradamus.  This journal was to be an annual retrospective on things occult, and included articles by most of the leading occultists of the period: Piobb, Oswald Wirth, Ch. M. Limousin, Papus, Alta, Annie Besant, Teder, Ernest Bosc, Ch. Lancelin, Paul Vulliaud, Jollivet-Castelot, C. Flammarion, "Jules Bois" (Henri Antoine Jules-Bois), and H. Durville, and many others.  The journal gave a full account (from Le Voile d'Isis) of the Spiritualist Congress held in Paris in 1907.  Apparently only two volumes were issued.
