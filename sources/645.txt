 First Divine Science Church Weekly Bulletins.
 1928--1937  Weekly
 Denver, CO.  Publisher: Colorado College of Divine Science.  Editor: Nona L. Brooks, D.S.D.
 Corporate author:  First Divine Science Church (Denver, Colo.)
 1/1, September 30, 1928-1937.  9 1/2 x 6.

 This was the weekly bulletin of Nona L. Brooks’ First Divine Science Church in Denver.   Unlike the Divine Science Weekly/Monthly, which were devoted more to the work of the Colorado College of Divine Science, the Bulletins were devoted to topical announcements, events, and the order of service for the current week (Prelude, Hymn, Medication, The Master’s Prayer, Response, Announcements, Bible Reading, Hymn, Offertory, Solo Hymn, Sermon, Doxology, Benediction), news and events.  Denver Public Library.     First Divine Science Church Weekly Bulletin 1928-1931 Scattered     </p>
