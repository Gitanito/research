 Sphinx, The.
 An Astrological Magazine.
 1899-1908?  Monthly
 Boston, MA; Chillocothe, MO.  Publisher: Sphinx Publishing Co..  Editor: Catherine H. Thompson.
 1/1, July 1899-1908(?).  $2.00/$3.00 a year.  72 pp.

 The advertisements for the journal in The Metaphysical Magazine, 1907, and in The Life, 1908, describe it as "the leading Astrological journal of the world, supported by the best writers.  It is entirely devoted to the Science of Astrology as taught by the Egyptians."  Contributions by Kymry, Sephariel, Astor, Merlin, Raphael, Aphorel, Cheiro, John Hazelrigg, Pallas, Ely Star, Asmothiel, Desbarolles, Heinrich Daath, et al.  Noted in "Astrological Serial Publications in the United States" in S.C. Gould's Notes and Queries and Historic Magazine 18, no. 2 (February 1900): 58-59.  LOC.
