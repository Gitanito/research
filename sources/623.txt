 Revue des Etudes Psychiques, La.
 Publication Mensuelle Consacree aux Recherches Experimentales et Critiques sur les Phenomenes de Telepathie, Premonition, Mediumnite.
 1898-1904  Monthly
 Paris, France.  Language:  French.  Editor: Cesare Baudi de Vesme.  Succeeded by: Annales des Sciences Psychiques
 1/1, 1898-1904.

 A second series was begun in 1901.  Baudi de Vesme (1862-  ) began this French edition of Rivisti di Studi Psichici when he became editor of Rivisti in 1898.  In 1905 the journal was merged into the Annales des Sciences Psychiques, of which Baudi de Vesme became editor.  BNF; ZDB: Freiburg Inst Grenzgeb Psychol; Munchen BSB.
